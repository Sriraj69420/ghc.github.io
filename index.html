GLOBAL HYPERLOOP COMPETITION

DREAM IT. BUILD IT. OWN IT.

BRINGING OUT THE BEST FOR THE FUTURE

Welcome to the Asia's first Hyperloop Competition happening on a global scale. This Annual competition involves teams around the world in a series of conferences, lectures, exhibitions to bring the best for realising the dream of Hyperloop. Focussing on bringing scalable technologies to develop the 5th mode of transportation, this competition takes place at the World's largest Student-run Hyperloop testing facility, namely the "Hyperloop Hub" at the Discovery Campus of IIT Madras, Thaiyur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
